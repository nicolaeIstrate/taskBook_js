The coordinates (x1, y1), (x2, y2), (x3, y3) of the triangle vertices are given. Find the perimeter and
the area of the triangle using the formula for distance between two points in the plane (see Begin20).
The area of a triangle with sides a, b, c can be found by Heron formula:
S = (p·(p − a)·(p − b)·(p − c))1/2,
where p = (a + b + c)/2 is the half-perimeter.
